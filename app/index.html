Singing Simply

Singing simply is a voice training tool that uses progressively more challenging exercises and songs to improve the pitch and rhythm of your singing. The exercises section contains exercises to train your musical ear (e.g. pitch, rhythm and interval recognition) and exercises to help improve your singing quality (e.g. singing a pitch/ interval/rhythm). The songs section gives you real-time feedback about your pitch when you sing along to one of the many available songs.
